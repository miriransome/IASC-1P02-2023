About

Welcome to my 'digital' art journal! What started out as a simple interest in art and being creative has now become a hobby. I use art as a form of relaxation when life gets too hectic and I feel the need to slow down, or if i just need a creative outlet. I'm still learning a lot of new things but this is a collection of some of the art that I have created over the years and feel comfortable sharing with the world. I hope you enjoy!

My art mainly consists of:

  1. Calligraphy
  2. Lettering
  3. Paintings
  4. Cards
