My Library

Reading is one of my favourite hobbies. I used to be an avid reader when I was younger and stopped as I got older and filled my time with other things. I got back into reading during the pandemic, and I’ve been in and out of it ever since. Other things still do take my attention from time to time, but I try to get in a good book when I can. So far, I’ve read 26 books for the year, so here are a handful of my favourites. I personally tend to read books based on if the covers are cute (or if it's been recommended to me by a friend or TikTok), so my faves will be displayed via their book covers. Accompanied with each book will be my rating and one of my favourite quotes from the book (click/hover over the book cover to reveal). I’m not going to leave my written reviews of the books because I don’t know how to give a good one without going into way too much detail.

P.S. – All of these are romance novels because that’s my favourite genre. Enjoy!

"She's pretty but her face doesn't transform into sunlight when she talks about music"

-Lynn Painter, Better Than The Movies

"I had a marvelous time ruining everything"

-Lynn Painter, The Do Over

"Maybe it's possible to have more than one home. Maybe it's possible to belong in a hundred different ways to a hundred different people and places"

-Emily Henry, Book Lovers

"My best friends taught me a new kind of quiet. The peaceful stillness of knowing one another so well, you don't need to fill the space. And a new kind of loud: noise as a celebration, as the overflow of joy at being alive"

-Emily Henry, Happy Place

"And I know that even if something did break you-because that's life and no one is invincible-you'd still put the pieces back together and remain the brightest thing I'd ever seen"

-Elena Armas, The Spanish Love Deception

"She holds my heart, and, with all that I am, I hope she never gives it back"

-Sarah Adams, The Cheat Sheet

"And I love him. For all of my memory, I have loved him"

-Laura Nowlin, If He Had Been With Me

"We're all a little broken, Briana. We are a mosiac. We're made up of all those we've met and all the things we've been through. There are parts of us that are colorful and dark and jagged and beautiful. And I love every piece of you. Even the ones you wish didn't exist"

-Abby Jimenez, Yours Truly
