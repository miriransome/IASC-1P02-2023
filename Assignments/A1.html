Trinidad and Tobago is a little country in the Caribbean made up of two sister islands. If you ask me, it's the best Caribbean country (but other people may disagree).

Trinidad is the more industrialized island whereas Tobago is more tourism based. Trinidad is where you'd go for the hustle and bustle of the city life, but if you want to wind down on a beautiful beach, then Tobago is the place to be. Some of the best things, people and food come from Trinidad and Tobago - Carnival, the steel pan, soca, calypso, parang, and doubles to name a few.
